My Projects

Explore the labours that I have completed in service of King Eurystheus, in penance for murdering my wife and children.

1. Slay the Nemean Lion

Killed the invulnerable lion by strangulation, and turned it's impenetrable skin into armour.

2. Slay the Lernaean Hydra

Defeated the multi-headed serpent by cutting off its heads and cauterising the wounds to prevent regrowth.

3. Capture the Ceryneian Hind

Captured the swift hind with golden horns and silver hooves without harming it.

4. Trapped the Erymanthian Boar

Trapped the massive boar that was terrorising the region, by driving it into a snowdrift.

5. Clean the Augean Stables

Cleaned the stables that had not been cleaned in 30 years, by diverting the rivers to wash them out.

6. Killed the Stymphalian Birds

Defeated the man-eating birds with a rattle and poisoned arrows.

7. Captured the Cretan Bull

Captured the wild bull that was terrorising Crete, by wrestling it to the ground.

8. Tames the Mares of Diomedes

Tamed the man-eating mares of King Diomedes by feeding them the flesh of their own master.

9. Acquired the Belt of Hippolyta

Acquired the magical belt of Queen Hippolyta, a gift from the Amazons.

10. Retrieved the Cattle of Geryon

Retrieved the cattle of the three-bodied giant Geryon, by slaying him and driving the cattle back to Mycenae.

11. Obtained the Golden Apples

Obtained the golden apples of the Hesperides, guarded by a dragon.

12. Stole Cerberus

Stole the three-headed dog Cerberus from the underworld, bringing it to the surface, and then returned them safely.
